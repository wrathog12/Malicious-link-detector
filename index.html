FAQs Section

What are Malicious links ?

Malicious links deceive users, leading to harmful websites or enabling fraud, malware, or phishing attacks. Users should exercise caution, verify links, and employ security measures to stay protected online.

What possible harm can malicious or spam links do ?

Malicious links can lead to a range of harms, including unauthorized access, data theft, financial loss, malware infections, identity theft, and compromise of personal or sensitive information

How to avoid malicious links ?

We have trained our model on numerous datasets, the site is pretty accurate but still tends to make some mistakes.

How do we determine whether a link is malicious or not?

Inspect URL, check domain reputation, use link scanners, verify HTTPS, consult blacklists, and employ user vigilance to assess link trustworthiness.

How accurate is our site ?

We have trained our model on numerous datasets so the model works pretty well but there is always room for few errors in every model.
